The planers of Islam

The pillars of Islam are the pillars that you must do in order to be a Muslim and Islam is only upright with them.

A testimony that there is no god but Allah, and that Muhammad is the messenger of God

Which is to testify that there is no god but Allah and that Muhammad Abdullah and his messenger are the messenger of the message

And doing the prayer

It is five prayers per day that pray on different shrines from today, Maghrib prayer, Isha prayer, Fajr prayer, noon prayer and Asr prayer.

And giving zakat

It means giving the prescribed zakat every year at some times of the year to the poor and poor relatives and others, and it is in a certain percentage.

And Ramadan fasting

It means visiting the Grand Mosque in Mecca and Medina and doing all the rituals and the pilgrimages

And doing the Hajj

And fasting is one of those who love deeds to God Almighty, where God Almighty said in a Hadith, all the work of the Son of Adam is for him except fasting, for it is mine and I am rewarded with it.
